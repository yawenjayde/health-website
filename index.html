Cucumer with Tuna Cream Cheese Dip

A healthy snack with only four ingredients! Cucumber, Tuna, Cream cheese, and Mayo. You could also add some opptional ingridents like sesame oil or hot sauce. Ready in 5 mins, a quick and easy snack!

Hey guys! Welcome to episode one of "Dont Burn Your House Down With Mr Dj Jay! Today we will be making a Tuna Cream Cheese Dip with Cucumbers as a side.

We will need:
- 1/2 Cucumber
- 1 tbsp Cream Cheese
- 1/2 tbsp Mayo
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp of furikake seasoning

Step 1: Preparing the Cucumber

Cut the cucumber into slices around 50mm and set aside

Step 2: Making the Dip

Add your cream cheese, mayo, and sesame oil into a small bowl. Mix it and top it off with furikake seasoning for a more aesthetic look.

Step 3: Finshing touches

Plate your chopped cucumbers and dip and serve. Yum yum in my tum tum.
